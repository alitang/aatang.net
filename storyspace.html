Story Space

(web design + ux/ui)

An interactive, location-based story sharing website that aims to create a culture of compassion for people of different backgrounds. Created in collaboration with designer Tiffany Choi and computer engineer Kelvin Lu.

Brainstorm

Story Space began as an intent to develop a platform that generates empathy for social issues. Tiffany and I brainstormed methods of raising awareness until we landed on the idea of using personal testimonials of social injustices. In researching websites of similar intents, we recognized the impact of location on generating empathy, as issues that “hit close to home” are more tangible.

Preliminary Prototypes

In developing the visual format of our platform, we took inspiration from websites like Humans of New York and I Remember to create a straightforward yet engaging platform.

Final Prototype

The last iteration was focused on making the platform more suitable for our target audience and more user-friendly. We re-evaluated our stylistic choices and made changes to create an overall sophisticated tone. We also included an instructional guide to create ease of navigation.
